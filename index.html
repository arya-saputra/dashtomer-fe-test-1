Brillian Gifted Midfielder

Addisou Mavumba is an exciting, technically gifted midfielder with natural flair and high football IQ. He’s known for his quick turns, creative vision, and calmness under pressure.

Addisou is widely regarded by local scouts as a top Senegalese talent with potential to break into a top European league in the next 2–3 years. He’s already on the radar of French clubs due to the historical pipeline between Senegal and Ligue 1 academies.
